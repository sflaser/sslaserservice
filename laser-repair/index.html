What to Send

The fastest repair review starts with identifiers and symptoms.

You do not need to solve the root cause before contacting us. Clear identifiers and a concise symptom summary are more useful than guesswork.

Minimum useful input

Application, platform, model, symptoms

Tell us where the system is used, the OEM or platform name if known, the visible model name, and what the system is doing right now.

Best supporting files

Labels, photos, alarms, and logs

Photos of labels, equipment sections, alarm screenshots, logs, and a short note on when the problem started are often enough to begin evaluation.
